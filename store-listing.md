## Full Description

NEET Live Buddy is your AI-powered study companion for NEET exam preparation. Powered by Google's Gemini 2.5 Pro AI model, it provides instant, detailed explanations for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology questions — in your preferred language.

**Key Features:**

- **Scan Questions**: Point your camera at any NEET question and get instant AI-powered explanations
- **Voice Input**: Speak your doubts in Tamil, Hindi, or English
- **Detailed Answer Format**: Every response includes the correct answer, option-by-option analysis, difficulty level, and NCERT chapter reference
- **Revision Cards**: Auto-generated concept summaries, key points, common traps, and practice questions
- **Multilingual**: Full support for English, Hindi (हिन्दी), and Tamil (தமிழ்)
- **NCERT-Aligned**: Answers are grounded in NCERT textbook content using RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation)
- **Voice Output**: Listen to explanations read aloud in your chosen language
- **Confused Mode**: Get simpler, more detailed explanations when you're stuck

**How It Works:**
1. Select your subject and language
2. Scan a question with your camera, speak your doubt, or type it
3. Tap "Ask Live Buddy" and get a comprehensive NEET-format answer
4. Review the revision card to reinforce your learning

**Built With:**
- Gemini 2.5 Pro for AI-powered explanations
- NCERT textbook knowledge base for accurate, curriculum-aligned answers
- Google Cloud Run for reliable, fast backend processing

Perfect for NEET aspirants preparing for the medical entrance exam. Study smarter, not harder!
