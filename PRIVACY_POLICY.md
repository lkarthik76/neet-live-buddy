# Privacy Policy for NEET Live Buddy

**Last updated:** March 14, 2026

## Overview

NEET Live Buddy ("the App") is an AI-powered educational tool for NEET exam preparation. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Information We Collect

### Camera Access
The App requests camera permission to allow you to scan exam questions. Images captured are:
- Processed locally on your device (resized and compressed)
- Sent to our backend server only for AI analysis
- **Not stored permanently** — images are discarded after generating a response

### Microphone Access
The App requests microphone permission for voice input (speech-to-text). Audio is:
- Processed using your device's built-in speech recognition
- Converted to text locally on your device
- **Not recorded or stored** by our servers

### Text Input
Questions you type or speak are sent to our backend for AI processing. We do not associate these queries with any personal identity.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers for tracking purposes
- Browsing history
- Contact information

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:
- **Google Gemini API** (via Google Cloud) — for AI-powered answer generation
- **Google Cloud Run** — for backend processing

These services are governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Data Security

All communication between the App and our backend uses HTTPS encryption. No user data is stored permanently on our servers.

## Children's Privacy

The App is designed for educational purposes and is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
